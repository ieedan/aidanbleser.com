---
title: AI Writes Most of My Code
description: Not a doomer take but an optimistic look at the future of AI and software development.
date: 2025-9-18
---

<blockquote>
	<p>I think we will be there in three to six months, where AI is writing 90% of the code.</p>
	<cite>— Dario Amodei, CEO and cofounder of Anthropic</cite>
</blockquote>

That's a bold claim made by the CEO of Anthropic, I think he was right. It's safe to say that today we are all feeling bearish about the capabilities of AI, recent launches like GPT5 have been marketed as game changers but really it's just more marginal gains.

But AI is writing 90% of my code, maybe even more.
